How to Make a PB&J Sandwich

  1. Get the bread, peanut butter, jelly and a knife.
  2. Use the knife to spread the peanut butter on the left side of the bread.
  3. Use the knife to spread the jelly on the right side of the bread.
  4. Put both pieces of bread together.
